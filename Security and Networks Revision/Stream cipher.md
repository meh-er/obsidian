#SaN 

**Stream cipher** is a symmetric key cipher than produces a ciphertext from plaintext by taking **one byte (8 bits) of plain text** at a time. Stream ciphers generate a (pseudorandom) **keystream** that is combined with teh plaintext to produce ciphertext. Stream ciphers are made for the scenarios where data needs to be encrypted in the continuous stream. e.g. RC4. Salsa20, ChaCha20
